Our Proven 6 Step Process

#1 Initial Consultion

Consult with us about the concept you want. Play the focus you want, and make your wishes come true

#2 Concept & Feasibility

Preliminary construction concept planning after exploring your needs and desires during the initial consultation.

#3 Planning & Coasting

After the concept is obtained, the next step is to plan the work and also project budget costs.

#4 Construction Stage

Construction stages are in accordance with any agreed items, in accordance with the work timeline and approved concept

#5 Finishing Touches

Finishing and repair process if there are defects at the construction stage. We strive to provide the best results.

#6 Cleaning & Decoration.

Cleaning construction or renovation project areas after construction work is completed, such as construction remains, etc.
